Catch Reports

Your catch history sells your fishery for you

When an angler finishes a session, Keepnet emails them at 3pm asking for a catch report. Species, weights, photos, conditions. Those reports build into a searchable public record — so the next angler deciding where to fish on a Saturday can look up your best peg in October, going back 10 years.

It gets them excited. It builds trust. It books the peg.
