Now — Beta

The core platform

📅

Online Bookings

Live peg availability, online payment, and instant confirmations. No phone tag, no double-bookings.

💳

Stripe Payments

Card, Apple Pay and Google Pay. Passed through at cost — no KeepNet markup, ever.

📲

QR Ticket Scanning

Anglers receive a unique QR ticket by email. Bailiffs scan at the bankside from any smartphone.

👥

Customer Records

A simple record for every angler who books — history, visits, notes.

🎣

Catch Reports

Automated 3pm prompt after every session. Species, weight, photos. Builds your searchable history.

📊

Owner Dashboard

All bookings, revenue and upcoming sessions in one view before you leave the car park.

Next — Summer 2026

Making it smarter

🗺️

Interactive Peg Maps

Anglers see and choose their exact peg on a map when booking. No confusion, no disputes at the bankside.

📋

Waiting Lists

Auto-fill cancellations from a waiting list. Popular pegs fill instantly — you do nothing.

📱

Bailiff App

A dedicated mobile view for bailiffs — scan queue, session list, flag issues, log incidents.

🏆

Match Management

Draw pegs, run results, publish leaderboards. For fisheries that run regular matches and competitions.

💬

Angler Messaging

Send session reminders, weather alerts, and fishery updates directly to booked anglers.

🎁

Membership Tiers

Season tickets, club memberships and syndicate access — managed and renewed automatically.

Later — 2027

The bigger picture

🔍

Angler Discovery

Anglers find your fishery through KeepNet's public catch map — searchable by species, region, and peg record.

⚖️

Legal & Compliance Tools

Rod licence checks, pollution incident logging, and access to specialist fishery legal support.

🎣

Tackle & Bait Partnerships

Exclusive deals for KeepNet subscribers from leading UK carp and coarse brands.

📈

Revenue Analytics

Seasonal trends, peg performance, and booking patterns — so you know where to invest next season.
