What Keepnet Does

Everything in one place

Five modules that work together — use one or use them all.

🎟️

Online Bookings & Day Tickets

Anglers book and pay online, 24/7. They get a QR code — your bailiff scans it on arrival. No cash, no faff, no missed revenue.

🪪

Memberships & Permits

Digital membership cards, automated renewal reminders, online joining, and waiting list management. All in one place.

🏆

Match Management

Digital peg draws, live weigh-in leaderboards, season league tables, and auto-generated match reports.

🐟

Fish Stock & Compliance

Log your stock, track stocking events, record catch returns, and export straight to Environment Agency format. DBS and safeguarding tracking included.

📊

Finance & Reporting

Real-time revenue dashboard, Stripe payouts, treasurer reports, and Xero/QuickBooks exports. Know your numbers at a glance.

📲

Bailiff & Angler Apps

Mobile-first apps built for outdoor use. Bailiffs scan QR codes and sell walk-up tickets. Anglers book, log catches, and follow match results.
