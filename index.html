The Problem

Running a fishery is hard work. The admin makes it harder.

Most commercial fishery owners are managing bookings by phone, handling cash at the bankside, and tracking customers in spreadsheets. There's a better way.

  • 📋
    Booking chaos

    WhatsApp, phone calls, Facebook DMs, and no-shows. Your pegs fill up by word of mouth and you have no visibility of what's booked until someone turns up.

  • 💵
    Cash at the bankside

    Cash handling is a security risk, an HMRC headache, and a time drain. Every transaction that moves online is time back in your day.

  • 🎣
    No catch history

    Anglers decide where to fish based on catches. Without a record, you can't show them what your waters produce — and you lose bookings to venues that can.

How It Works

The KeepNet Loop

Every feature feeds the next — on a flat monthly fee, with all data owned by you.

01
Angler

Books online & pays instantly

Live peg availability. Card, Apple Pay or Google Pay. No phone calls, no WhatsApp, no cash on arrival.

02
KeepNet

Sends a unique QR ticket by email

Confirmation is instant. The QR code is tied to the booking — it cannot be shared, reused or faked.

03
Bailiff

Scans at the bankside

Any smartphone. No app, no signal needed. Valid = green. Duplicate or unpaid = flagged. Cash leakage closed. Included in the flat monthly rate — no extras.

06
The loop closes

Catch history drives the next booking

New anglers search your catch records before booking. After one season you have a marketing asset. After three, it's a moat.

05
KeepNet

Builds your searchable catch history

Reports accumulate by lake and peg. Visible to future anglers. Controlled by you. Gets more valuable every season.

04
After the session

Angler gets a catch report prompt

At 3pm, KeepNet emails them automatically. Species, weight, photos. No chasing. No forms. It just happens.
