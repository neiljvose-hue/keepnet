The Problem

Running a fishery is hard work. The admin makes it harder.

Most fishery owners are managing bookings via Facebook Messenger, memberships in spreadsheets, and match results by hand. There's a better way.

  • 📋
    Booking chaos

    WhatsApp, DMs, phone calls, and no-shows. Your pegs fill up by word of mouth and you can't see what's happening at a glance.

  • 🗂️
    Member records everywhere

    Memberships tracked in spreadsheets, renewal reminders sent manually, fee chasing eating your evenings.

  • 🏆
    Match management headache

    Drawing pegs, recording weights, calculating results — all done by hand, with plenty of room for error and disputes.
